Choosing the Perfect Logo

After much debate within our team, we carefully selected a logo that represents the simplicity of our platform and welcomes everyone. The icon emphasizes the educational aspect, while the platform's name attaches the financial dimension to our mission.

Our slogan, "A Educação Financeira divertida" (The Fun Financial Education), invites viewers to explore a new and enjoyable approach to a traditionally complex topic.

Our Font Choice: Poppins

Font Used: Poppins

Commercially available and chosen for its conveyance of simplicity, excellence, and reliability simultaneously.

Poppins is a versatile and modern typeface that offers a clean and sophisticated aesthetic. Its extensive font weights and styles make it suitable for various design elements, from headers to body text.

We selected Poppins to align with our brand's values of clarity and approachability. The font's readability and elegant design contribute to creating a positive and engaging user experience.

2E4B61: A shade of dark blue that conveys trust, stability, and professionalism. It can be associated with the seriousness and confidence people seek in financial matters.

3EE3A9: A mint green suggesting freshness, growth, and harmony. It can be associated with personal and financial development, creating a sense of innovation.

45C97D: A more vibrant green that communicates vitality, prosperity, and success. Reinforces the idea that the platform can boost users' financial growth.

4CAF50: A more traditional green representing stability, security, and growth. It can be associated with financial success and decision security.

F1E05A: A soft yellow that adds a touch of energy and positivity. Represents clarity and optimism associated with good financial education.

EBA144: An orange shade that evokes enthusiasm, energy, and creativity. It can add an element of innovation to the brand.
