Our Solution to Address the Lack of Financial Literacy

A user-centric platform tailored to their needs. Providing content that users want to learn and easily accessible information. Offering a personalized journey based on their interests.

The Key Differentiator - Finance 101 Experience

The most distinguishing factor of Finance 101 is the user experience it provides. It goes beyond being a web application with articles and financial literacy courses. After users register and complete a questionnaire assessing their profile, knowledge, preferences, and needs, they are guided through a Custom User Path (CUP). This path is segmented into sections containing articles and quizzes.

All basic and free articles are always accessible to all users, emphasizing our commitment to providing introductory financial education for everyone. The platform incorporates gamification with a points system, competitions, and rewards to engage users, even those initially disinterested in financial literacy. Users can give feedback through likes and comments, allowing us to track opinions and make improvements accordingly.

Main Features Using Gamification:

  • • Points system earned by reading articles, completing sections, and answering quizzes.
  • • Customization of user profiles with rewards, characters, and other items.
  • • Streaks for consecutive days and a leaderboard to enhance competitiveness.
  • • Upcoming feature: an investment simulator where users can apply and multiply points/coins.
  • • Option to purchase points with real money.
