Hello world! Finance 101

Finance 101 started in early September 2023 when a group of students was challenged to create an innovative project. We decided to create a real solution to a real problem, and thus, the Finance 101 project was born.

Vision

We aspire to create a society where financial literacy is a universal competence. We envision a future where every Portuguese, regardless of their background, age, or location, has easy access to solid financial knowledge and understands the complexities of the economic world. By empowering contemporary young minds with the necessary tools to make informed financial decisions, we are building a more stable, prosperous, and equitable tomorrow for everyone in Portugal.

Mission

Empower the Future: At Finance 101, we are dedicated to revolutionizing financial education for the Portuguese. Our mission is to create an intuitive web application, filled with interactive learning paths, quizzes, and gamified experiences. We aim to make the complex world of finance and economics accessible, enjoyable, and fun, fostering a financially literate generation prepared for a prosperous future.
