Our Story

Welcome to Finance 101, where innovation meets financial education. In September 2023, a group of students embarked on a journey to create a real solution to a real problem. Thus, the Finance 101 project was born.

Our Vision

We envision a future where financial literacy is universal. Regardless of background or location, every individual should have easy access to solid financial knowledge. By empowering minds with the tools for informed decisions, we strive to build a stable, prosperous, and equitable tomorrow for all.

Our Mission

Empowering the Future: We revolutionize financial education for the Portuguese. Our intuitive web application, filled with interactive learning paths and gamified experiences, aims to make the complex world of finance enjoyable. Join us in fostering a financially literate generation prepared for a prosperous future.
